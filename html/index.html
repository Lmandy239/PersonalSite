Answer to 3-B

One significant hurdle was the need to test the back end of my projects, which prompted me to delve into concepts like JSON, request bodies, headers, and understanding status codes. This exploration was crucial to enhance the functionality of my applications. Authentication became another area of focus, although this was more of a front-end testing on new angular servers rather than back-end logic. To test all this stuff, I took the initiative to learn about Postman and its features. This tool proved to be useful for testing the functionality of my applications and the security measures. Additionally, the transition from utilizing JPQL and JPA to embracing RESTful services presented a noteworthy challenge. Previously relying on DAOs and crafting my own logic for methods, I had to adapt to using the JPArepository implementation and properly creating methods within the specific repositories. Moreover, although not directly related to back end. For my apps to look nice and clean, I had to learn frameworks such as Angular and Bootstrap. Which I think highlights my ability to learn new stuff that can make my products much better and viable.

Another particularly rewarding experience involved working on the Recipe Recommender project. Initially, we used arrays to create a user's cart to store ingredients. However, this would not persist the choices. Meaning I had to figure a way to make it happen. Therefore, I undertook a thorough investigation to implement proper many-to-many relationships. Which is how I was able to connect the tables on MySQL Workbench and then map the proper entity relationships.

These challenges, ranging from mastering new technologies like JSON and Postman to adapting to RESTful APIs and tackling complex database relationships or learning Angular and Bootstrap reflect my enthusiasm for pushing the boundaries of my knowledge. They underscore my commitment to staying abreast of the latest developments in the field and my eagerness to apply this knowledge to create innovative and efficient solutions – qualities that I personally believe align with the requirements of the role.
